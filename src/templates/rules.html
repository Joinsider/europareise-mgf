Rules

Start Game:

Create a Game:

  1. To create a game start on the Startpage and click on the Create Button. You will then get to a site on which you have to enter the number of players.
  2. After that you will be asked to enter a username you like.
  3. Now you see a Game ID which you have to give to the other players after that you have to wait for the other players to join the Game.

Join a Game:

  1. To join a game you have to enter the Game ID you got from the player who created the game.
  2. After that you will be asked to enter a username you like. And now the game starts.


Gameplay:

Start:

  • You will start in a random location on the map. Search for your marker which has the same color as your name on the top.
  • The player, that created the game will start.
  • If it's your turn then roll the dice by clicking on the button in the top right.

Goal of the Game:

  • The goal of the Game is to visit 8 European cities and get back to your starting location.
  • Your starting and target location are marked with a star.
  • You can only visit your starting location if you have visited all other locations.

Move:

  • After you rolled the dice you can move your marker by clicking on the location you want to move to.
  • You can only move to locations that are connected to your current location.
  • There are different forms of travelling possible:
    By Land:
    This is the normal type of movement and can be done if the connection has a solid line
    By Sea:
    This type of movement can be done if the connection has a dotted line
    This can only be done if you are at a city with a port (dotted connection)
    If you have moves left you can wait at port and in the next round you can only travel to your destination city
    By Air:
    This type of movement can only be done on major (red) cities
    You can see the sectors by clicking on the little plane button in the corner
    There is also a difference between flights within one sector and outside:
    1. Flights within one sector cost 2 moves
    2. Flights outside of one sector cost 4 moves
